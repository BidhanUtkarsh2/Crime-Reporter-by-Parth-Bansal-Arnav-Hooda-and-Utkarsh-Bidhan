Understanding Crime in Delhi NCR

The Delhi National Capital Region (NCR) encompasses Delhi, Noida, Gurgaon, and surrounding areas, serving as India's political, economic, and cultural hub. Like many major metropolitan areas globally, Delhi NCR faces complex challenges related to crime and public safety. This project examines these issues through a balanced, data-driven approach to understand the types of crimes, their trends, and their impact on society.

Our research combines statistical analysis, public perception surveys, and anonymous community reporting to create a comprehensive understanding of the crime landscape. This educational project aims to inform students and educators about crime prevention, community engagement, and policy implications
